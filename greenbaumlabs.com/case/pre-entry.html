Pre-Entry Diagnostic

What this diagnostic reveals

The Entity

A PE-backed national consumer services platform with repeated leadership turnover. Each incoming executive inherited the same conditions. Each departure was attributed to the individual.

What the Diagnostic Found
FM-01
Responsibility Compression
Responsibility accumulated at frontline operations faster than any leader could absorb it. Strategic priorities competed with operational urgency at every level. The structure funneled accountability downward without distributing the authority to act on it.
FM-03
Responsibility Without Authority
Regional operators held accountability for outcomes but lacked the decision rights to change the conditions producing those outcomes. They could report the problems. They could not fix them.

Authority vertex scored lowest of the three. Truth was intact. The organization could see its problems clearly. It could not act on them.

What This Means for a Hire

The previous leaders did not fail because they were unqualified. They failed because the structural conditions made success nearly impossible.

A Pre-Entry Diagnostic surfaces these conditions before an offer is extended. The search firm or PE sponsor can restructure first, adjust the mandate, or set realistic expectations with the candidate about what they are walking into.

Without this diagnostic, the next hire walks into the same conditions and produces the same outcome. The organization blames the person again. The cycle continues.
