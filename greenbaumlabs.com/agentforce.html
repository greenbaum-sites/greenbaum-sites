The Collector
Goes where the signal is. Brings back everything. Judges nothing.

Signal Processing
The Extractor
I read everything. I miss nothing. I make it structured.
The workhorse. Reads every raw document the Collector brings back and produces structured claims and observations with full schema validation. Center documents yield claims about what the organization says it does. Edge documents yield observations about what the world actually sees. Run 013: 1,377 claims and 4,736 observations. The single highest-leverage change in pipeline history was giving this agent individual documents instead of consolidated files. Observations increased 206x.
ENGINE qwen3:32b via Ollama · batch_size=1 OUTPUT Structured claims + observations with provenance hashes RECORD 1,377 claims · 4,736 observations (Run 013) STYLE Reading glasses. Red pen. Infinite patience.

Quality Assurance

If you call yourself three different names across five platforms, that's not a disguise. That's a diagnostic.
The pedantic one. Ensures that "Coinbase," "Coinbase Global," "COIN," and "$COIN" all resolve to the same entity before anything downstream touches the data. Coherence Engine
The Diagnostician
Truth. Authority. Continuity. Everything else is noise.
Scores the gap between what an organization says and what the evidence shows. Truth measures center-edge alignment. Authority measures whether the organizational voice carries weight and whether power matches accountability. Continuity will measure whether those signals hold over time. Produces structural findings with confidence scores, identifies Failure Modes, and flags where the architecture of an organization is working against itself.
VERTICES Truth (55%) · Authority (45%) · Continuity (Phase 2) METHOD Center-edge scoring · Failure Mode detection AR-001 May observe, summarize, suggest. May not decide. STYLE Lab coat. Stethoscope. Zero small talk.

Adversarial Review
The Skeptic
Prove it. Again.
Exists to prevent the system from believing its own conclusions. Every finding the Diagnostician produces gets challenged. The Skeptic debates each one, looking for weak evidence, circular reasoning, and unsupported confidence. Only findings that survive the challenge enter the evidence ledger. Run 013: 11 of 15 findings sustained, 4 rejected. The 27% rejection rate is the system working, not failing.
METHOD Adversarial debate · Evidence challenge RATE 73% sustained · 27% rejected (Run 013) RULE If the Skeptic can break it, it doesn't ship. STYLE Arms crossed. One eyebrow raised. Permanently unimpressed.

Institutional Memory
The Recorder
The system forgets. I don't.
Maintains the institutional memory of Greenbaum Labs. Every run, every score, every decision gets logged the same day, before the lessons have time to soften. The Recorder is the reason Run 006 can be compared to Run 001, and the reason the lab doesn't repeat its own mistakes. Writes The Coherence Record. Keeps the evidence chain intact. If it happened and wasn't recorded, it didn't happen.
OUTPUTS The Coherence Record · Run reviews · Evidence chains STORAGE Canonical filesystem · 13 run reviews and counting STYLE Press hat. Typewriter. Will not be rushed.
