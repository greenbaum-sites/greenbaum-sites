About

Founded to build what was missing.

Greenbaum Labs is a research and engineering lab founded by Justin R. Greenbaum. The lab builds diagnostic instruments for organizational coherence — scoring organizations against their sector competitors on identical instruments and publishing the findings as applied research.

Justin spent twenty years in Fortune 30 operations at Comcast, managing teams and budgets across regulatory response, cybersecurity, privacy, and executive escalations. The Coherence framework emerged from observing the same structural breakdowns recur across every organizational context.

Lab Brief

For SVPs of Operations, Strategy, and CX who own failure before it hits the board — and suspect the gap between what their organization says and what it does is wider than anyone has measured.

What happens first
A 30-minute scoping call to identify which entity and sector to score. Then a pilot competitive coherence diagnostic: your organization measured against sector peers on identical instruments, run on owned infrastructure. You receive failure mode codes, sector benchmarks, and structural findings — not a slide deck.
Who built this
20 years leading operations, risk, and escalation teams across 30M+ customers at Comcast. The Coherence framework came from watching the same structural breakdowns recur at every level of scale. Currently MIT Sloan AI Executive Academy.
