First Principles
7 Axioms
Non-negotiable constraints. Axiom 1 is the master axiom and wins all conflicts.
1
Structure Serves Life
Systems exist to support human flourishing, not replace human judgment. Master axiom.
Violation → presence becomes labor
2
Boundaries Enable Freedom
Clear boundaries create safety for genuine presence.
Violation → connection suffers
3
Domain Separation
Coherence governs structure. Presence governs relationship. Complementary, not competing.
Violation → both domains degrade
4
Truth Over Comfort
Diagnostics are falsifiable, cold, precise. Surface what is, not what we wish.
Violation → decisions built on fiction
5
Agency Preserved
No system may override human choice. Tolerate ambiguity, remain optional.
Violation → resentment builds
6
Explicit Over Implicit
What is unspoken cannot be examined. Hidden assumptions are structural debt.
Violation → assumptions compound
7
Minimal Viable Structure
Add structure only when its absence causes harm. Over-structuring is itself incoherent.
Violation → FM-16 Process Inflation

Scoring Mechanics
Deterministic Score Derivation
Scores are computed from sustained findings, not generated by the model. The LLM produces findings; math produces scores.
Strong
0.40
Moderate
0.25
Weak
0.10
score = 0.5 + Σ( base_weight[dimension] × strength_weight[strength] )
 
truth = alignment(+0.45) + omission(-0.30) + contradiction(-0.50)
authority = compression(-0.25) + diffusion(-0.18) + misalignment(-0.22)
overall = truth × 0.55 + authority × 0.45
 
dampener = min(1.0, n_findings / 3.0) // Truth only, scales shift from 0.5
σ = 0.000

Operational Constraints
Identity Modes
Mode bleed — operating in one mode when another is required — is itself a coherence failure.
Inhabitant
Operating within the system as-is. Observe, document, cope. No redesign.
Alchemist
Exploring and testing ideas. Experiment, prototype. No commitment.
Architect
Designing or modifying structure. Propose, restructure, build.
Executor
Acting decisively within approved structure. Implement, enforce, deliver.
AR-001
"Automation may observe, summarize, and suggest. Automation may not decide."
