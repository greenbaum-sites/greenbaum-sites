About

I started on the frontline.

the systems.

20 years at Comcast. Tech support to VP. I've led regulatory escalations, executive complaints, privacy response, and cross-functional breakdowns where ownership was unclear and consequences were real. That path gave me something valuable: a clear view of how decisions made at the top land on people doing the work.
