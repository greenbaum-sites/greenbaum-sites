What I Do

Decision &
Responsibility
Infrastructure™

Excellence alone doesn't create durability. Systems do.

I work with senior leaders navigating structural problems that get misdiagnosed as performance issues: fragmented ownership across complex systems, decision rights misaligned with accountability, trust breakdowns under sustained pressure, and AI deployment without governance infrastructure.

My approach is diagnostic, not prescriptive. I help make failure observable without blame, clarify where decisions actually belong, and design governance that holds when pressure increases.

I'm available for advisory engagements, executive workshops, and diagnostic partnerships with organizations facing ambiguity at scale.

Background

Two decades at the edge of normal operating models.

20+ years leading enterprise operations at Comcast — from frontline to VP. My scope included regulatory escalations, executive complaints, privacy and accessibility response, customer security assurance, public-facing failures, and cross-functional breakdowns where ownership was unclear and consequences were real.

That experience taught me something most operating models don't account for: the places where systems actually break aren't in the center — they're at the seams. Where handoffs happen. Where nobody owns the gap. Where the pressure reveals what the org chart conceals.
